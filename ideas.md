# Design Ideas for COVID-19 Depression Research Website

## Response 1

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Data Journalism / Editorial Brutalism
**Core Principles:**
- Raw data as the hero — numbers and statistics are displayed at large, typographic scale
- High contrast black-and-white base with a single electric accent (deep crimson #C0392B)
- Asymmetric grid layouts that feel editorial, like a printed research journal brought to life
- Dense information hierarchy with clear typographic rungs

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0D0D0D) background with white text and crimson (#C0392B) for critical data points. The starkness communicates urgency and seriousness.

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset grid — sections alternate between full-bleed and constrained columns. Large pull-quote numbers (e.g., "25%") float at massive scale as section anchors.

**Signature Elements:**

- Giant typographic statistics as section dividers
- Horizontal rule separators with label tags (like a printed report)
- Monospace font for data labels, serif for body

**Interaction Philosophy:** Scroll-triggered number counters; sticky section labels that update as you scroll.

**Animation:** Fade-in + slide-up for text blocks; count-up animation for statistics.

**Typography System:** Display: Playfair Display Bold 80px; Body: IBM Plex Serif 18px; Data labels: IBM Plex Mono 14px
</text>

## Response 2

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Clinical Modernism / Medical Data Dashboard
**Core Principles:**
- Deep navy (#0A1628) background evoking authority and depth
- Crisp white typography with electric orange (#3B82F6) accents
- Clean grid structure with generous whitespace — feels like a premium medical journal
- Data visualizations embedded inline as first-class content

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy conveys trust, authority, and the gravity of the subject. Electric orange accents guide the eye to key data. White text ensures readability. A subtle teal (#06B6D4) is used for secondary highlights.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width dark hero with centered headline, followed by alternating two-column and full-width sections. Sticky top navigation with smooth scroll anchors.

**Signature Elements:**

- Glowing stat cards with subtle border gradients
- Section dividers using thin horizontal lines with centered label text
- Plot images displayed in dark-bordered frames with captions

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth scroll navigation; hover states on stat cards reveal source citations.

**Animation:** Entrance animations using framer-motion (fade + slide); stat numbers count up on scroll into view.

**Typography System:** Display: Space Grotesk Bold 72px; Body: Inter 18px; Labels: Inter Medium 14px uppercase tracking-widest
</text>

## Response 3

<response>
<text>
**Design Movement:** Academic Broadsheet / Ink-on-Paper
**Core Principles:**
- Warm off-white (#F5F0E8) background evoking archival paper
- Dark charcoal (#1C1C1C) text with a single muted gold (#B7860B) accent
- Multi-column editorial layout inspired by academic journals and broadsheet newspapers
- Generous use of serif typography and ruled lines

**Color Philosophy:** The warm paper tone grounds the research in tradition and credibility. Gold accents reference academic achievement and citation. The overall palette feels timeless rather than trendy.

**Layout Paradigm:** Three-column grid on desktop that collapses to single column on mobile. Pull quotes span two columns. Figures and plots are displayed with ruled borders and numbered captions.

**Signature Elements:**

- Ruled column dividers
- Drop caps on section openers
- Numbered figure captions in small caps

**Interaction Philosophy:** Minimal interaction — the content is the focus. Footnote-style hover tooltips for citations.

**Animation:** Subtle fade-in on scroll; no distracting motion.

**Typography System:** Display: Playfair Display 64px; Body: Lora 17px; Labels: Lora Italic 14px
</text>
